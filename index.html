This is where I’m trying to store useful medical nuggets. I’m going to try and split them into a few different types of page:

Recipes
How-to articles - e.g. “Recipe: Investigating AKI” - a no-nonsense checklist of things to do in a situation
Understand
How-to-think articles - e.g. “Understanding causes of AKI” - more detailed articles about the thought process that should go into a particular problem.
Notes
Possible disorganised collections of notes and references about a topic. Mainly for me.
