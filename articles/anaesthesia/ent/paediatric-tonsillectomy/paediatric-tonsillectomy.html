Pre-op

Not much to say. Worth gauging indication: recurrent infection vs OSA (?doesn’t make as much difference in kids as adults)

Intra-op

Induction

Gas or IV both ok. Can augment a gas induction with IV bolus of opiod+propofol once canulated.

Airway management

Many, many ways to skin a cat…

  1. “Trad”: paralysis + tube (south-facing RAE)
  2. Tube without paralysis…
    1. Get them deep and just do it - best with kids < ~7 + not too fat
    2. Topicalise the cords (but need to be NBM for ?1hr after)
  3. LMA: flexi-LMA - arguably protects glottic opening better + facilitates spontaneous ventilation

Emergence

Important: Beward the coroner’s clot - always flex the neck supine + suction for nasapharyngeal blood.

Post-op

Usual paracetamol/NSAID/weak opiod combo.
